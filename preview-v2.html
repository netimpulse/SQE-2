Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What types of solar systems do you inspect? +
We inspect residential, commercial, and utility-scale solar PV systems. Our expertise covers crystalline silicon, thin-film, and emerging PV technologies.
How long does a thermal inspection typically take? +
Thermal inspection duration depends on system size. A 5-10 kW residential system typically takes 2-3 hours, while larger commercial systems may require 1-2 days.
Do you provide reports with recommendations? +
Yes, all inspections include comprehensive reports with detailed findings, thermal images, and prioritized recommendations for maintenance or repairs.
What is the cost of your inspection services? +
Pricing varies based on system size, location, and scope. We provide custom quotes. Contact us for a free consultation and detailed estimate.
How do you measure energy yield losses? +
We use advanced modeling software combined with thermal imaging data to identify and quantify energy losses from soiling, faults, and degradation.
Are your inspectors certified? +
Yes, all our inspectors are certified thermographers, IEC specialists, and hold relevant industry qualifications including NABCEP and ISO certifications.
